Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ZOMET AI is a digital product. Once your license key has been delivered, we generally do not offer refunds. Please read the exceptions below carefully before purchasing.

1. No Refund After Key Delivery

Because ZOMET AI is a digital product and the license key is delivered immediately upon successful payment, we do not offer refunds once the license key has been issued and sent to your email. This is standard practice for digital software licenses.

2. Eligible Refund Conditions

A refund may be considered in the following situations:

  • Duplicate payment — you were charged more than once for the same order
  • License not delivered — your payment was confirmed but no license key was received within 24 hours
  • Product not functioning — the application fails to work as described and the issue cannot be resolved by our support within 3 business days

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us via WhatsApp within 3 days of your purchase date. Please include:

  • Your order ID or email address used at checkout
  • Proof of payment (screenshot or transaction ID)
  • Description of the issue (if applicable)

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after the license key is delivered
  • License key suspended due to a violation of our Terms & Conditions
  • Failure to use the product within its validity period
  • Issues caused by incompatible hardware or operating system not meeting the minimum requirements

5. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 3–7 business days to your original payment method. Refund timelines may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

6. Contact

For refund requests or questions, contact us via WhatsApp. We aim to respond within 1 business day.
